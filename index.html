ABOUT

Radio Remedy is a 4 piece rock cover band from Melbourne playing the best classic rock and funk songs from the 70’s to today. Just the remedy for live music to get your event rocking.

The band consists of:

  • Michael Gibson - Lead Vocals & Rhythm Guitar
  • Jason Sandford - Lead Guitar & Backing Vocals
  • Dean McKeown - Bass Guitar & Backing Vocals
  • Mihoko Sato - Drums & Percussion
